What is Krav Maga Juniors?

The Krav Maga Juniors program is designed to teach practical and effective self-defense in a fun, dynamic, and encouraging environment. Krav Maga differs from traditional martial arts in that it stresses function over form; and our Krav Maga Juniors program is designed to help students achieve a high level of proficiency in the system in a relatively short amount of time. In addition to self-defense, the Juniors program offers your child the opportunity to learn, develop, and practice the knowledge and skills needed to be physically fit and mentally strong.

Class information

Classes are open to students at all levels of skill and fitness, and are designed to help take your junior to the next level.

Fun, Dynamic Warm-up

Functional fitness

Krav Maga training

The heart of the program, Krav Maga training introduces students to self-defense techniques that will improve their personal safety

Character building
